01 — Core Concept

What is Ideation?

Ideation is the process of generating creative ideas to solve real problems. The first step is always to understand the problem clearly before thinking about solutions.

During ideation, we create many ideas without judging them — even small or unique ideas can become big innovations. Asking questions like "Why?", "How?" and "What if?" sharpens our thinking.

Team discussion gives different perspectives and better solutions. Testing and improving ideas makes them stronger. Ultimately, ideation teaches creativity, confidence, and open-minded thinking.

From 2D to 3D

2D shapes have only length and width, while 3D shapes add depth and volume. A square becomes a cube. Converting 2D into 3D makes objects look more realistic and easier to understand.

This topic improved my visual imagination and helped me understand how real-world objects are constructed and perceived.

How to Make a Kite?

A kite must be light but strong. The frame uses two thin sticks in a cross shape. Balance is critical — without it, the kite won't fly. The tail provides stability and the string must be tied at the correct point to catch wind. Concepts like air pressure, lift, and wind direction all apply.

02

Time Management

A daily schedule helps complete work on time. Clear goals make it easier to focus. Avoiding distractions like mobile phones saves time. Prioritizing important tasks improves productivity and reduces stress. Time management teaches discipline, responsibility, and smart working habits.

03

Sleep

Proper sleep is vital for health and learning. Good sleep helps the brain work better, improves concentration, supports growth and recovery. Avoiding screens before bed improves sleep quality. It also reduces stress, keeps the mind fresh, and improves memory and study performance.

Health
04

Gut Health

The gut plays a vital role in digesting food and absorbing nutrients. Eating fruits, vegetables, and fiber improves digestion. Drinking enough water supports gut health. A healthy gut strengthens the immune system and keeps the body active and energized.
