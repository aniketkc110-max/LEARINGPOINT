My learning journey through the Zero2Maker program — exploring ideas, design, science, and life skills that shaped the way I think and create.

Learning Points
01 — Ideation

Creative Problem Solving

Ideation is generating creative ideas to solve real problems. I learned to understand the problem first, create many ideas without judgment, and ask powerful questions like "Why?", "How?" and "What if?" — building creativity, confidence, and open-minded thinking.

02 — Design

2D to 3D Thinking

2D shapes have length and width; 3D shapes add depth and volume. Converting 2D into 3D makes objects realistic and easier to understand — used in design, animation, gaming, and engineering. This topic sharpened my visual imagination.

03 — Research Paper

How to Make a Kite?

A kite must be light but strong. The frame uses two thin crossed sticks for support; balance and tail placement keep it stable. I explored air pressure, lift, and wind direction — learning creativity and patience through hands-on making.

04 — Life Skills

Time Management

Managing time is key to success. Daily schedules, clear goals, avoiding distractions, and prioritizing tasks improve productivity and reduce stress. Time management teaches discipline, responsibility, and smart working habits.

05 — Wellness

The Importance of Sleep

Proper sleep helps the brain work better, improves concentration, memory, and performance. Sleeping on time, waking early, and avoiding screens before bed builds a healthy routine — teaching discipline, balance, and self-care.

06 — Health

Gut Health

The gut digests food and absorbs nutrients. A balanced diet with fruits, vegetables, and fiber — plus enough water — supports digestion, strengthens the immune system, and keeps the body active. Healthy eating is a lifelong habit.
